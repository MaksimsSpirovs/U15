About Us

This website is a representative of the ‘Hot Beans Web’ company which primarily specialises in IT support, including installations of workstations and management of systems. The company is in active search of prospective employees, like certified IT Technicians to assist the company in management of its systems, training of employees and self-development in their respective fields. It is our priority that we make the website as easily usable as possible to all kinds of users – we do not discriminate against our potential employees with factors that might be limiting them and their abilities. Our target demographic is individuals of any age and gender that have completed IT training, and have a history of courses and qualifications in relation to Technical Support and expertise with hardware. Such requirements allow us to ensure that we hire the most qualified and capable employees to prospect our company. The pursuit of our website is to attract both experienced IT Technicians to assist in the development of our company, as well as not so experienced in IT people that are nevertheless interested in the company – for the second party our website is going to feature numerous IT courses which will allow for more direct and streamlined teaching of our companies aims and prospects, giving the users enough knowledge to be able to develop and grow on their own experience in our company.
